About Me

Ever since I picked up a Super Nintendo controller at the age of five, I’ve been passionate about the intersection between storytelling and technology.

Before web development, I’ve explored this passion in everything from gaming to digital art to creating a choose-your-own-adventure story that was featured in UCF’s 2012 Knights Write showcase.

More recently, I’ve put my passion to work as a freelance website writer and inbound marketer, translating the stories and value of businesses into website and landing page copy, blogs, sales emails, white papers, and other online content.

I can’t get enough of creative thinking and self-learning, so all of my non-freelance work experience comes from small companies that required its employees to constantly learn new skills and pitch in when another department (sometimes one person) got overwhelmed. This is where I thrive.

I’m not just a web developer--I’m a storytelling, web writing, online marketing, full stack web developing problem solver who would love to work with a company that needs employees who aren’t afraid to step out of their job descriptions and take on new challenges.
